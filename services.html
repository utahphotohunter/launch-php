Our Services

Day Program

Our community based day program gets out every day. Staying at home all day every day ins't healthy for anyone. If we wouldn't accept it, why should our clients? Lets get out there!

Group Residential Homes

Our group residential homes are places where our clients live with roommates just like a regular apartment. We staff them according to client needs making sure to give the best care possible. While at home, our staff take care to help teach our residents life skills, meeting them where they are. We work with client goals to make a plan just for them. They help do chores around the house and make decisions on activities and things like what to eat. It's just like living with friends!

Host Family Homes

Host homes are families or individuals that some of our clients live with full time. They are become family, going on outings, and pitching in as one of the crew. Clients always have their own rooms. Many have jobs they go to during the day and all are supported in their goals and needs.
